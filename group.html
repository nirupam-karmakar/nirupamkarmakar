About TROPICS

The TROPICS Research Group investigates the dynamics, predictability, and future evolution of the tropical climate system. Our research combines observations, theory, numerical modeling, Earth system models, and artificial intelligence to understand weather and climate variability across timescales ranging from days to decades.

Current research themes include monsoon dynamics, intraseasonal oscillations, air–sea interaction, extreme rainfall, low-pressure systems, Earth system modeling, and machine learning for weather and climate prediction.

Teaching Highlights

  • Designed and taught graduate-level courses in climate dynamics and variability, statistical methods, and atmospheric processes.
  • Developed Python-based computational laboratories for climate data analysis, numerical modeling, and visualization.
  • Integrated observational datasets, reanalysis products, and Earth system model outputs into classroom instruction.
  • Promoted reproducible scientific computing using Jupyter notebooks and open-source software.
  • Mentored graduate students through course projects, research dissertations, and summer internships.

Teaching Resources

  • Course materials, announcements, assignments, and discussions for enrolled students are managed through Google Classroom. Public teaching resources, including selected lecture notes, Python notebooks, and tutorials, will be made available through this website and my GitHub repository.
